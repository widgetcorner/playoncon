A Play On Con companion app — Beta

Play On Con is a tabletop and gaming convention held each year at the Alabama 4-H Center in Columbiana, Alabama — four days of theater, RPGs, board games, video games, parties, and the outdoors. This app is a pocket-sized guide to the weekend, currently in Beta.

Designed offline-first. The schedule and venue map work without a network connection, so you're covered in the cabins, the Lodge, and anywhere on the property where signal gets thin.

WHAT'S IN THE APP

• Full schedule, all four days — every session across every venue, opening to the current time slot so you always land on what's on now.

• My Schedule — save the sessions you don't want to miss.

• Reminders — local notifications before any saved session, at start time or a custom number of minutes ahead. No account, no push server.

• Venue map with "you are here" — color-coded pins for Stages, Gaming, Parties, Outdoors, and Stay & Eat. Tap any pin for what's happening there. Location is opt-in.

• Show on map — jump straight from any session to its venue pin.

• Session tags — quick badges for Ages 21+, PG-13, Apprentice Track, Auditioned/Casted, Sensory Friendly, and Sign-up at Open Gaming.

• Discord and sync status — quick link to the community and a clear last-synced indicator.

PRIVACY

No account. No login. No ads. No tracking. The app downloads the published schedule and keeps a local copy. Location is opt-in and never leaves your device. See https://fullerc.github.io/playoncon/ for the full privacy policy.

ABOUT PLAY ON CON
